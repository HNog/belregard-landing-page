A Crude Medieval Horror Tabletop Roleplay Game


What is Belregard?

A Roleplay Game setting with the crude brutality of the dark ages, horrors of unknow nightmares who live in the profane alcove fed by the sins of man; mysteries and tribulations of simple people in a decaying world, where evil (The Living Shadow) already won, god, is dead!

"Humans, for the good and the bad..."

A book inspired by the works of the great masters: H.P. Lovecraft, Bernard Cornwell, Robert E. Howard, Kentaro Miura, Umberto Eco... and more!

Taking as Core Rule Set the acclaimed Generic Medieval System in Brazil: Crônicas-RPG by Pedro Borges.

"Quick to learn, deadly and very roleplay wise"

Belregard is not only a setting for your darkest campaigns, is food for the lovers of the horror and rustic medieval genre.



Who is it for?

Dungeon Masters - Belregard provides you with plenty of materials to build your campaigns in this crude and horrifying scenario. You will build your own dark world!

Players and other enthusiasts - Here your characters can experience those awesome scenes, plots and histories your mind created when reading the books of the genre!



How can I acquire it?

Sadly, Belregard is not readily available in English YET! But you can support the project by joining the growing community and the great authors: Jefferson Neves and Rafael Araujo, on their Facebook Group.

For those who speak Portuguese, the pre-order is available in the New Order Editora.
